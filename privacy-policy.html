Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Tasbih (the "App") handles your information. Your privacy is critically important to us.

1. Information We Collect

Tasbih is designed to be a private tool. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, usage data, or device information to any external servers.

All data, including your prayer counts and settings, is stored locally on your device and is not accessible by us.

2. Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services that collect your data.

3. Permissions

The App may request minimal permissions (such as haptic feedback/vibration) solely to enhance your user experience. These permissions are not used to collect data.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at: support@tasbih.app
